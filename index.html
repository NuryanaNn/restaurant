our menu

Whether you’re in the mood for a hearty burger or want to surprise your taste buds with a zesty seafood entree, Geogeske’s menu will entertain every palate. Pair up your steak with a glass of your favorite red from Geogeske’s ample wine selection. To top things off, Geogeske offers a delicious selection of homemade desserts and only brews Illy coffee.
